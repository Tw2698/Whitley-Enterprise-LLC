Dr. Timothy A. Whitley

Founder and CEO of Whitley Enterprise LLC, Dr. Timothy A. Whitley is an audio visual engineer, educator, author, musician, consultant, and technology leader with decades of experience in professional systems integration, media production, education, and leadership development.

A Legacy Built Since 1999

Founded in 1999, Whitley Enterprise LLC was established with a vision to combine technical excellence, creativity, education, and innovation. The company provides professional solutions in audio visual engineering, technology integration, consulting, publishing, music production, and training.

Dr. Whitley's career has been shaped by extensive experience in live sound, broadcast systems, recording environments, networking, electrical systems, military aviation maintenance, and professional education.
